This E-Voting system will help you to cast your valuable votes using uses electronic means. As information technology evolves over time, the need for a better, faster, more convenient and secure electronic voting is an essential requirement since there are various drawbacks associated with the traditional voting technique. Traditional paper-based voting can be time consuming and inconvenient. Electronic voting not only accelerates the whole process, but makes it less expensive. Therefore, through this application we will try to overcome all the security issues by allowing voters to cast their votes in the absence of any unfair means, to increase voter confidence and improve the voting experience as well as provide accurate results by solving the case of vote miscounts.
